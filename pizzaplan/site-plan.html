Purpose

Have you ever been at home and had a craving for Pizza?

Most people in this situation decide to buy a pizza to be delivered, but that can be expensive and unhealthy.

For a healthier, cheaper option, I present to you Pizza Plan, the go-to website for all things pizza.

On Pizza Plan, you can find recipes for all different kinds of pizza, including dairy and gluten-free options as well as

other add-ons to make your pizza the way you enjoy it. Don't rely on a premade greasy pizza to cure your craving,

make your own the way you want it!

RECIPE -(The same for dairy and normal pizza) DOUGH 2-2 1/3 cups of all-purpsoes flour or bread flour 1 packet of instant yeast (2 1/4 teaspoon) 1 1/2 teaspoons sugargar 3/4 teaspoon salt (optional)garlic powder to taste (1/8-1/4 teaspoon) 3/4 cup warm water

PIZZA Sauce (same for all three pizzas) 6 oz tomato paste 15 oz tomato Sauce 1-2 Tablespoons of dried oregano 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning 1/2 teaspoon garlic poweder 1/2 teaspoon onion powder 1/2 tablespoon garlic salt 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper 1 teaspoon sugar

Dough- Gluten gluten-free 1 1/2 cups (234g) King Arthur Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour* 2 tablespoons (18g) buttermilk powder 1 teaspoon baking powder 3/4 teaspoon table salt garlic poweder to taste (1/8-1/4 teaspoon) 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast 1 tablespoon granulated sugar or honey, optional 1 cup (227g) water, warm

Steps:Dough for dairy and normal pizza 1) Mix 1 cup of flour, yeast, sugar, salt and garlic powder 2) Add warm water and stir very well. 3) Gradually add another 1 cup of flour. Add additional flour if needed until the dough pulls away from the side of the bowl. 4) Allow dough to rise for 30 minutes or until it has doubled in size. 5) Roll out the dough and transfer it to a pan. Pinch the edges to create a crust 6) Use a fork to poke holes in the dough. 7) Add sauce and toppings evenly distributed around the pizza. 8) Set oven to 425F and cook for 13-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.

Steps: Gluten Free Dough 1) Combine yeast and 3/4 cup of warm water. Mix and allow the yeast to activate for 5 minutes. Sprinkle 1 TBSP of sugar after few minutes 2) In seperate bowl, combine flour, salt,backing powder and the rest of the sugar. Mix till combined 3) Combine yeast mixture and dry mixture. Add olive oil and 1/2 cup of warm water and stir well till combined add more flour if necessary. 4) Let set for about 15 minutes to let set and rise 5) Roll out the dough and put sauce and toppings on toppings 6) Heat oven to 350F and cook for 20-25 minutes. The crust will may have cracks in it, this is normal don't worry.

Steps: Pizza Sauce 1) In a medium bowl, mix the tomato paste and tomato sauce 2) Add all seasonings into the mixture, stir till combined

TOPPING OPTIONS Cheese(mozzeralla) Bacon Daiya Cheese (Dairy free cheese substitute) Canadaian Bacon Sliced Olives Italian Sausage Spinach Sausage Chopped onion Hot dogs(My roomate tried it once, said it wasn't bad) Sliced tomato Garlic Sauce (Recipe Bellow) Chopped Bell pepper Chopped Mushrooms Pineapple (your despicable) Artichoke

Steps: Garlic Sauce 1) Melt 1 TBSP of butter 2) Mince 1 Clove of garlic and add to the melted butter 3) Add 1/2 teaspoon of dried parsely to the metled butter, mix well 4) Spread butter mixture on the dough before adding toppings and (optional) on the crust after its cooked.
