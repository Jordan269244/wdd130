Pizza Sauce

  • 6 oz tomato paste
  • 15 oz tomato Sauce
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of dried oregano
  • 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic poweder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Mix all ingredients together in a medium bowl and enjoy!

Gluten Free Dough

  • 1 1/2 cups (234g) King Arthur Gluten-Free All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 tablespoons (18g) buttermilk powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon table salt
  • Garlic poweder to taste (1/8-1/4 teaspoon)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar or honey
  • 1 cup (227g) water, warm

Steps

  1. Combine yeast and 3/4 cup of warm water. Mix and allow the yeast to activate for 5 minutes. Sprinkle 1 Tbsp of sugar after few minutes
  2. In seperate bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder, and the rest of the sugar. Mix till combined
  3. Combine yeast mixture and dry mixture. Add olive oil and 1/2 cup of warm water. Stir till combined. Add more flour if necessary.
  4. Cover for about 15 minutes to let set and rise.
  5. Roll out the dough to desired shape and put on sauce, toppings, and cheese.
  6. Heat oven to 350F and cook for 20-25 minutes. The crust may have cracks in it, this is normal don't worry.

Dairy Free Dough

  • 2 cups (234g) All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 packet of instant yeast (2 1/4 teasppon)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon table salt
  • Garlic poweder to taste (1/8-1/4 teaspoon)
  • 2 Tablespoons vegetable
  • 1 cup (227g) water, warm

Steps

  1. Mix 1 cup of flour, yeast, salt and garlic powder
  2. Add warm water and oil and stir very well.
  3. Gradually add another 1 cup of flour. Add additional flour if needed until the dough pulls away from the side of the bowl.
  4. Allow dough to rise for 30 minutes or until it has doubled in size.
  5. Roll out the dough and transfer it to a pan. Pinch the edges to create a crust
  6. Use a fork to poke holes in the dough.
  7. Add sauce and toppings evenly distributed around the pizza.
  8. Set oven to 425F and cook for 13-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.

Steps: Garlic Sauce

Optional

  1. Melt 1 TBSP of butter, a non-dairy substitute will also work
  2. Mince 1 Clove of garlic and add to the melted butter
  3. Add 1/2 teaspoon of dried parsely to the metled butter, mix well
  4. Spread butter mixture on the dough before adding toppings or (optional) on the crust after its cooked.
