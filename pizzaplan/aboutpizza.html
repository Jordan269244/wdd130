About the Cook

Hi! I am sophmore in college and am doing this website for a class asignment. I have always loved cooking and have gotten very good at it over the time I have lived away from home. My roomate, a really good friend of mine, is allergic to dairy. There are several meals that she doesn't have the pleasure of eating, including pizza. I decided to create this website to help those who have diet restrictions. I hope you enjoy my recipe and that the people who have gluten or dairy restirctions will finally have the amazing meal of pizza!
